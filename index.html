On Smell

Exploring olfactory language, memory, and description.

About

This site collects short, sensory-first descriptions and invites you to play with smell-based language.

Manifesto (excerpt)

During the winter break, upon returning home, my grandmother made me my favorite dish — pork rib and lotus root soup. The “taste of home” I remembered is gradually dissolving along with her forgetting. Smell is an embodied memory difficult to capture with words; this site aims to foreground olfactory descriptions and the language-games that keep them alive.
