Manifesto 2.0

Contemporary research often concludes that olfactory language is declining, but much of that evidence comes from WEIRD populations. In contrast, less industrialized groups like the Maniq and Jahai retain complex, abstract olfactory vocabularies that guide daily life. Smell, as an as-yet "undigitized" sense, has been marginalized in our visually dominated information society. The suppression of non-visual senses and exclusion of non-dominant communities creates deep epistemic biases. Manifesto 2.0 proposes reintroducing smell-description into language games so AI can become more inclusive of people, perceptions, memories, and emotions traditionally left out.
